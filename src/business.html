Our basic process for multi-tiered support

 

    • Configuring a connection;
    • Integration;
    • Preparation of the base;
    • Configuring reporting.
    • Recruitment;
    • Writing scripts and training;
    • Education;
    • Refreshing.
    • Appointment of leaders of the group;
    • Scheduling of the agents;
    • Testing agents;
    • Control of agents.
    • On-line monitoring;
    • Additional training;
    • Quality specialist.
    • Work time: day shifts or 24/7/365;
    • Increase of leaders at all workplaces;
    • New skills and additional training;
    • Multichannel: Web-chat, Skype, mail, IVR.
    • Sustainable process;
    • The constant presence of the team leader, surveillance, reporting, online statistics.

An outsourcing services is about long-term trusted relationship. In order to prove our capabilities and make smooth inception we offer to start from a pilot project for 3-5 people and see how it works.
We also give very special conditions for every new customer.
